Our Foundations

Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To cultivate critical thinking, strategic planning, and intellectual resilience in individuals of all ages. Through chess coaching, FIDE ratings, and high-standard tournaments, we nurture community leaders and chess champions.

Our Vision: To remain a globally recognized center of excellence in chess instruction and event staging. We believe chess is a vehicle for cognitive development and deep logical mastery, accessible to every curious mind.

Foresight

Building the capacity to look 5-10 moves ahead, both on the board and in real-life operations.

Cognition

Enhancing problem-solving speed, spatial intelligence, and memory retention.

History

Our Tournament Legacy

Staging elite events and producing highly rated champions for over two decades.

1998

Founding & Board Inception

Established as an independent association with 4 wooden chess boards in a cozy library hall. Dedicated to teaching chess to local school pupils.

2008

National Club Championship

Represented the city in the National Chess Arena, securing gold in the rapid and classical brackets. Membership scaled to 200+ active chess players.

2018

FIDE Grand Prix Hosting

Selected as a host venue for the regional FIDE Grand Prix, drawing 40 international title holders including 12 GMs and 18 IMs.

2026

Launch of Digital Chess Classroom

Equipped clubrooms with interactive DGT sensor boards, streaming rigs for tactical analyses, and access to cloud-based neural engines.
