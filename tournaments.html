Getting Ready

Tournament Preparation Guide

Everything you need to know before competing in a Chess Masters event.

Pre-Tournament Checklist

  • Register online at least 7 days before
  • Bring valid ID and FIDE rating card (if applicable)
  • Arrive 45 minutes before first round
  • Bring notation sheet and approved pen
  • No electronic devices in playing hall
  • Review current FIDE rules (updated annually)

Mental Preparation

  • Get adequate sleep the night before
  • Practice time management with clocks
  • Review your opening repertoire
  • Solve 10-15 tactical puzzles daily
  • Stay calm and focused during play
  • Analyze losses with a coach afterwards
