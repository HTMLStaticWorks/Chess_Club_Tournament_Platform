Frequently Asked Questions

Read standard answers about clubrooms, FIDE updates, and pricing policies.

Do I need a FIDE ID to play tournaments?

No. If you do not have a FIDE ID, our arbiter board will allocate you a local ID during first round pairings check-in and submit your name to the federation for standard rating registration.

Can I cancel or pause my membership plans?

Yes. You can pause or cancel your monthly subscriptions at any point directly from the accounts office or by sending an email cancellation request 5 days prior to billing updates.

What equipment should I bring to tournaments?

All sets, electronic digital clocks, DGT sensor boards, and notation booklets are supplied directly by the club. Players only need to bring legal check-in documents.
