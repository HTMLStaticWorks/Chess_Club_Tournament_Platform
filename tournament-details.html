Rules & Regulations

Notation Requirements

Standard algebraic notation is mandatory. Sheets must be updated move-by-move. Failure to maintain a notation sheet leads to loss of claiming draw rights.

Default / Late Policy

Players must arrive at the board within 30 minutes of the round start. Beyond 30 minutes, the match is defaulted and the opponent is awarded 1 point.

Anti-Cheating Safeguards

No electronic devices, smartwatches, or phones are allowed in the tournament area. Random scans will be executed before round pairing check-ins.
