History

Club Milestones

A chronological look at our progress over the decades.

Club Founded

1998

The club was founded by a coalition of international arbiters to build chess culture in London.

First Grandmaster Produced

2006

Student Mark Davies achieved his final GM norm at the London Chess Invitational hosted in our halls.

FIDE Affiliation Granted

2012

Formally recognized by the International Chess Federation to submit tourney results directly for rating updates.

Interactive Clubhouse Opened

2021

Established the modern luxury clubrooms featuring digital screens, interactive tables, and streaming rooms.
