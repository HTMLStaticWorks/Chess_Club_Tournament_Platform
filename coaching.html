Chess Coaching Pathways

Structured coaching, interactive study circles, and personalized grandmaster mentoring tailored to accelerate your progression.

BEGINNER

1. Rules & Basics

Designed for novices to structure board vision. Learn coordinates, standard rules, piece actions, value structures, and simple 1-2 move checkmating nets.

  • Piece movements & board coordination
  • Basic tactical motives (forks, pins)
  • Under-1000 ELO target goals
Book Path
INTERMEDIATE

2. Strategy & Positional Play

For players who understand basics but struggle with long term plans. Study major opening configurations, pawn skeletons, and essential endgame theories.

  • Opening layouts & standard defense patterns
  • Positional valuation & weakness tracking
  • 1000 - 1600 ELO target goals
Book Path
COMPETITIVE ELITE

3. Grandmaster Repertoire

Preparing candidate masters for standard FIDE events. Focus on deep calculating trees, psychological preparation, and database engines integration.

  • Computer engines training (Stockfish/Lichess)
  • Complex theoretical endgames (Rook + Pawn)
  • 1600 - 2200+ ELO target goals
Apply for Elite
JUNIOR SCHOLAR

4. Youth Championship Program

Cultivating high-tier focus and cognitive habits in players under 18. Structured group leagues, homework puzzles, and school tournament prep.

  • Highly engaging puzzle sheets
  • Collaborative class tournaments
  • Under-18 tournament prep courses

One-on-One & Workshops

For direct, personalized attention, our International Masters (IM) and Grandmasters (GM) are available for private 1-on-1 consultations in-person or digitally.
